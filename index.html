Institutional Ethos

At Narsingdi Government Girls High School, we are committed to nurturing a learning environment that inspires intellectual vigor, moral fortitude, and an enduring passion for serving the community. Our institution seeks to develop students into well-rounded individuals equipped to excel in all facets of life.

Admissions Overview

Prerequisites: Completion of primary education.
Application Process: Forms available both at the school office and online.
Selection Criteria: Based on academic merit and competitive entrance examinations.

Academic Paradigm

Our curriculum is tailored to meet national standards, offering a balanced educational experience in the Sciences, Humanities, and Commerce. We aim to cultivate analytical skills, a sense of curiosity, and an appreciation for lifelong learning.

Extracurricular and Enrichment Programs

  • Performing Arts: Showcasing talent in drama, dance, and music.
  • Sports and Athletics: Encouraging participation in football, volleyball, and more.
  • Debate Society: Fostering eloquence and critical thinking.
  • Science and Innovation Club: Stimulating scientific curiosity through experiments.
  • Literary Circles: Engaging in creative writing and poetry recitations.
  • Eco-Club: Promoting environmental awareness and action.
  • Art Workshops: Exploring various artistic mediums and techniques.
  • Community Service Initiatives: Cultivating a spirit of altruism.

Principal's Message

Our esteemed Principal, Md. Abdul Ahed, emphasizes academic rigor and the holistic development of each student. His leadership embodies a vision for a brighter future through quality education.

Campus Resources

  • Extensive Library: A vast array of books and reference materials.
  • Science Laboratories: Cutting-edge facilities for practical experiments.
  • IT Lab: Equipped with modern technology to enhance digital literacy.
  • Sports Grounds: Facilitating comprehensive physical education.
